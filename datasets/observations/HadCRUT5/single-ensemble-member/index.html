HadCRUT5: Single ensemble member¶

Monthly temperature anomalies (w.r.t. 1961-90) from one ensemble member of the HadCRUT5 dataset. The vertical axis is latitude (south pole at the bottom, north pole at the top), and each pixel is from a randomly selected longitude.¶
